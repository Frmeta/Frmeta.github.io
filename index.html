About Me

I am a 19-year-old Game Developer from Indonesia. I am a passionate and dedicated game developer with a strong focus on creating innovative and captivating gaming experiences. My journey in the world of game development has been largely driven by my participation in various game jams, where I've had the opportunity to work on a variety of projects, mostly on a solo basis. These experiences have not only honed my technical skills but also nurtured my ability to think creatively and adapt quickly to new challenges. Here you can see some of the projects i've been making, either solo or as a team.

These examples from my game jam portfolio illustrate my passion for crafting games that are both fun to play and visually appealing.
