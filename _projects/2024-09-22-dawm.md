Doni's Adventure with MAS is a 3D side-scrolling educational platformer where players travel across the diverse islands of Indonesia to document its unique biodiversity. Players have to identify native flora and fauna (such as the Komodo dragon or Rafflesia arnoldii) to populate a comprehensive digital Almanac filled with real-world ecological facts. The game ends with large-scale Leviathan boss fight that tests player's platforming agility.
